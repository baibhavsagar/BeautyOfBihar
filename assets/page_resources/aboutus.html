Hello, I'm Baibhav Sagar, a Computer Science professional from Patna, Bihar. I recently completed my Master's in Computer Application (MCA) from the Indira Gandhi National Open University, following a Bachelor's in Computer Application from LNMI, Patna.

My skills span programming in C/C++ and Java, web development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Node.js, database management with SQL and NoSQL, and cloud computing with AWS, Azure, and IBM Cloud. I've deployed a No Code pipeline on Azure and have experience with Linux/Unix, Docker, and Ansible. Certified in web development, blockchain essentials, cybersecurity, and IoT, I bring a diverse skill set to the table.

During my Front-End Developer Internship at Shymmetric Technology, I led the development of a Student Learning Platform. Explore my portfolio for more details on my projects and skills. I'm excited to connect and explore new opportunities in technology.
